Welcome To
Goenka Group

Goenka Group is one of the leading groups in Nepal with diversified business involved in both manufacturing and trading of various goods. The group started importing grocery items from India in 1963, the first business commencement of the group in Nepal and thereafter the group has rigorously diversified its business into different fields. Today, the group has two manufacturing units: Goenka Steels (P) Ltd. and Goenka Cement (P) Ltd., the only steel and cement units located adjacent to each other at Bhairahawa, Rupandehi, Nepal.
